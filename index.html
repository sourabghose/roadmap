Your 9-Month Career Roadmap

From Beginner to ML/AI Engineer

Journey Overview

This roadmap is structured into three distinct phases to guide your transformation. Each phase builds upon the last, ensuring a solid foundation before you tackle more advanced concepts.

  • 1

    Data Analyst Foundations (Months 1-5)

    Master the core skills of data manipulation, analysis, and visualization using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

  • 2

    Data Scientist Transition (Months 6-7)

    Dive into machine learning, learning to build and evaluate predictive models for real-world problems.

  • 3

    ML/AI Engineer Transition (Months 8-9)

    Focus on engineering principles by deploying models, understanding MLOps, and exploring advanced deep learning.

Phase 1: Data Analyst Foundations

This foundational phase is all about learning the language of data. You'll master Python for programming, SQL for database communication, and essential tools like Excel and Power BI for analysis and visualization.

Phase 2: Data Scientist Transition

Here, you'll transition from analyzing the past to predicting the future. This phase introduces you to the core concepts of machine learning, from foundational algorithms to building and evaluating your own predictive models.

Phase 3: ML/AI Engineer Transition

The final phase bridges the gap between a model on a laptop and a product in the real world. You'll learn how to deploy models, manage their lifecycle (MLOps), and explore the advanced deep learning architectures that power modern AI.
